Privacy Policy for VR Backgrounds (Foggy Californian Beach)

1. Introduction

VR Backgrounds (Foggy Californian Beach) (the “App”) is a virtual reality application available on the Meta Quest platform. The App creates an immersive experience by placing users in a seamless, looped video environment. At VR Backgrounds, we respect and protect user privacy, and this policy describes how information associated with your use of the App on Meta Quest is handled.

2. Data Collection and Use

VR Backgrounds (Foggy Californian Beach) does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The App does not require registration and does not gather usage data, location data, or other personal information. Any data necessary for the App’s functionality remains solely on the user’s device and is not transmitted externally or to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services and Data Collection by Meta

VR Backgrounds (Foggy Californian Beach) is distributed exclusively through the Meta Quest platform. Please note that Meta may collect certain information in accordance with its own privacy policies as part of your use of the Meta Quest platform. 4. Data Security

Because VR Backgrounds (Foggy Californian Beach) does not collect or store personal data, there are no data security risks related to personal information. All data necessary for the App’s functionality is handled locally on the user’s device.
